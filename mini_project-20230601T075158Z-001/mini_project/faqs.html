A: Regular check-ups are essential for maintaining your pet's health. We generally recommend annual wellness exams for adult pets. A: In the event of a pet emergency, please contact our clinic immediately. If it occurs during our business hours, we will provide guidance and arrange for prompt medical attention. For after-hours emergencies, we can refer you to a local 24-hour emergency veterinary clinic.
